Please follow these rules to maintain a fair and friendly game and community.

## No cheating or abuse

Do not use cheats, modified clients, exploits, or any form of cheating. Violations may result in a ban depending on severity.

## Be respectful

No harassment, insults, hate speech, or discrimination. Be civil and respect other players and staff.

## No spam or ads

Do not spam chat or post unauthorized advertisements or referral links.

## Accounts and resources

No account trading, boosting, or sharing. By using this site you accept that we may ban or take other measures to enforce order.

## Reporting

If you see someone breaking the rules, report to staff via QQ or Discord. We will review and take action when appropriate.
